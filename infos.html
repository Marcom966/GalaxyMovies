Movie Galaxy

HOW TO USE THIS WEBSITE

Use the button on the left of the page to search for informations about any film you like.
Use the button on the right to search for films released in one specific year.
You can also search for a specific year by clicking on the year of realease of one movie of your choice.
Use the numeric buttons to discover more movies released with the same title.
If you want to know more about one movie click on the "more details" button.
A window will open in front of you with further details about the film you are searching for.
Click on the movie's name to seach for a trailer on youtube.
click on the movie's image to search for a Soundtrack Album on Spotify.
Click on the year of realease to search for more movies released on the same year.
Once you're done, click on the "close" button in the bottom-right of the window.
NOTE: in order to use the search for year of release functionality you have to specify a movie title on the
"search by title" button of this website.
